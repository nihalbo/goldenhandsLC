The Problem

  • In a survey conducted by the U.S Bureau of Labor of Statistics, only about 24.9 percent of American residents volunteered in 2016 (the most recent year available)
  • The percentage of people in the United States that volunteer is declining
  • Not enough people are volunteering

The Fix

  • Our website game-ify's the volunteering experience, it consists of:
    • A way for everybody (you don't have to register) to find nearby volunteering opportunities and tasks (such as gathering food for homeless shelters), depending on the specfic person's age and interests
    • A continuous system that encourages its users to volunteer through the application of onine badges and leaderboards stats (at the user's wish)
    • A way for its users to donate to other NPO's through our website
    • A seperate volunteering competition focused more rigorously on Loudoun's youth named HRS4KIDS
